Cum a apărut GirlsGoIT

Programul GirlsGoIT a fost lasat în martie 2015, la Forumul de pre-lansare în localitatea Mingir din Republica Moldova.

GirlsGoIT’s platform is one of UN Women’s key priority agenda for Moldova which is “to promote gender equality and the economic empowerment and advance Women and Girls’ access to ICT in Moldova[1]” Since GirlsGoIT was launched in March 2015, UN Women office in Moldova have fully dedicated support for the mission of GirlsGoIT and because of UN Women’s support, GirlsGoIT has become a national program established in 13 regions in Moldova building future STEM ambassadors to pique interest in mainstream technologies.
